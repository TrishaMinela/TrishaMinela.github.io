Join the Chamber

Small Business

The Calauan Chamber of Commerce puts you at the top priority. We want to help businesses and self-employed individuals grow, create more jobs, and support the city's economy. Our chamber is working to create more resources and connections for you. We recognize your part in the livelihood and culture of the city. Join today to receive the benefits we offer!

Chamber

Joining the chamber means you are joining a business organization representing businesses and companies within the city of Calauan. You will advocate for businesses, business policies, ethical resources, and for the city's economy. You will be dedicated to support the growth of the businesses within the chamber. Belong to the organization today!

Small Business Membership

Non Profit

Access to articles, subscription to email updates, access to local virtual events, and discounts on selected businesses.

Bronze

Access to articles, subscription to email updates, access to local and provincial virtual events, discounts on selected businesses, access to virtual conferences, and participation on job fairs.

Silver

Access to articles, subscription to email updates, access to local and provincial virtual events, discounts on selected businesses, access to virtual conferences, participation on job fairs, opportunity to be selected for school internship programs, access to meetings with government and non-government leaders, and discount on LBC.

Gold

Access to articles, subscription to email updates, access to local and provincial virtual events, discounts on selected businesses, access to virtual conferences, participation on job fairs, opportunity to be selected for school internship programs, access to meetings with government and non-government leaders, discount on LBC, part of the voting to be an official member of the chamber leadership, advertisements and promotions, and exclusive connections with businesses within the province.

Chamber Membership

Signature

Subscription to monthly newsletters concerning businesses, Shell Membership Discount, and access to leadership virtual events and conferences.

Advantage

Subscription to monthly newsletters concerning businesses, Shell Membership Discount, access to leadership virtual events and conferences, discount on Chamber services, leadership voting rights, leadership running rights, access to trainings, and access to national connections.

Elite

Subscription to monthly newsletters concerning businesses, Shell Membership Discount, access to leadership virtual events and conferences, discount on Chamber services, leadership voting rights, leadership running rights, access to trainings, access to national connections, direct access to government bodies and leaders at a national level, opportunity to become part of the board of directors and/or chamber committee and, monthly presidential letter for the board of directors.

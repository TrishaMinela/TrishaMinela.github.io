Local Companies

TJZED Cafe

TJZED Cafe is a family owned business that started in 2020. It used to be a milkshake stand turned into a Cafe. It is located at Calauan's main highway, Bangyas Highway. This road is taken if going to Manila or Sta Cruz. The Cafe serves burgers, pizzas, milkshakes, and more!

Morales Resort

Morales Resort recently just opened during the pandemic. It is not widely known yet but they describe the place as a "Nature Farm Resort".

Isdaan

Isdaan floating restaurant has been elevated as a higher experience restaurant. The traditional nipa huts are floating in a man made body of water. This restaurant is often compared to restaurants in Thailand. They offer traditional Filipino food and their service is impeccable.

Calauan Chamber of Commerce

The Calauan Chamber of Commerce seeks to promote tourism and business within the city by utilizing the members of the commerce's profession. We advocate for the protection, growth, and influence of the businesses within the city. We work to provide resources, connections, and a platform for businesses. Collaborations and associations are important to the chamber to achieve synergy within the city.

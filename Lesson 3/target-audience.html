Target Audience

The South Forecast website will be designed for those working citizens and travellers to the South Luzon areas. The demographic will be those in their 20s-50s. The people that are busy in their career or those that are taking a break from their career to travel. These are the people who constantly check for weather updates as it will affect their day or experience.

Who: In field workers, travelling workers, local citizens and interprovincial travellers.

Age: 20-50

Technology: Phones, tablets or ipads and laptops

Income: P140,000-P200,000

Motivation: Be informed on weather forecasts for the day or the coming week.

Personas

Lanie Sanchez

Occupation: Wedding Photographer

Demographics and Education: 29 years old. She is single and living in Manila. Graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship.

Goals and motivation for using the site: Travels often to South Luzon for wedding shoots and checks the weather in case of a need to change in venue.

Social: Has a lot of friends that she hangs out with in different provinces of South Luzon when she is not working.

Technology: Often uses her phone only to contact clients and schedule appointments which includes checking the weather on her phone.

Quote: "Working as a freelance photographer means I manage everything on my own. I need to ensure I am prepared for such things like the weather conditions."

Name: Chris Velanzuela

Occupation: Landscape Architect

Demographics and Education: 42 years old. Has been married for 9 years and has 2 children. He has lived in South Luzon only since he got married. Has a Bachelor's degree in landscape architecture

Goals and motivation for using the site: Travels occassionally to sites and checks daily for a weather forecast.

Social: Often travels within South Luzon with his wife and 2 children.

Technology: Mostly checks everything on his laptop when he uses it for work.

Quote: "In my day to day job, it involves travelling to clients, contractors and sites. To ensure my safety on site and on the road, ensuring the weather condition is a safety habit."

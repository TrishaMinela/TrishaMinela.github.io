The goal of South Forecast is to provide weather and climate information in the south region of the Luzon island. It is know that this region mostly experiences rainy or dry weather, however, the population seeks to be safe from typhoons and extreme storms. Weather forecast will also be helpful to assist with the protection of society from everyday activities that the weather can have an impact on. The website will advocate the protection of life and property through educating on the significance of the influence of weather.
